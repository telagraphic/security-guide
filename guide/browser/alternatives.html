Brave

Brave was founded by the Brendan Eich, the creator of Javascript (the language of the browser). Brave has privacy and security built into the browser. Other browsers will require multiple extensions to get the same funtionality as Brave.

  • Ad Blockers
  • Cookie Blocking
  • Fingerprinting Protection
  • HTTPS Everywhere
  • Phishing/Malware Blocker
  • Private Search with DuckDuckGo!
  • Experimental TOR Browser
