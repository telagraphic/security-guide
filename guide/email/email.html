Email

Google used to read your email, granted this was an automated process done by computers but it is illegal in the U.S. to read someone else's mail. Google has dropped this practice, but it says a lot about how they view users.

If you still want to use gmail, they do have top security teams that make gmail very secure, then check out their Advanced Protection
