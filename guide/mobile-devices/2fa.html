2 Factor Authentication

2 Factor Authentication, 2FA, is simply a second password, a backup password to verify your first password. It is safeguard against someone else using your credentials. Many popular sites and apps are implementing it .

2FA comes in a couple of different flavors. SMS Text sent to your phone are now considered unsafe. Even NIST has called out SMS too easy to intercept .

The preferred approach is to use an authentication application on your phone. It generates random numbers that sync to an account.

2 Factor Authentication is a complete 'must have' these days, so set it up on your phone for all accounts that support that .
