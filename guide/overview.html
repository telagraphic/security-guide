    Internet Service Provider

    Your ISP tracks you and sells your data. Learn what you can do about it.

    Browser

    Learn about new privacy browsers, extensions to block ad trackers and bust the myths of private browsing.

    Domain Name System

    DNS is the phone book of the Internet. Prevent data leaks that reveal your search and IP address.

    Search Engines

    Use an alternate search engine that doesn't track you. Most of them use google under the hood but strip out data that can be used to identify you.

    Virtual Private Network & TOR

    Understand how a VPN can increase your privacy and how to choose one. Most are scams!

    Router

    Level up your router security with a checklist from a computer expert. The most important part of this guide!

    Passwords

    Passwords are out, passphrases are in. Learn about password managers to make life easier and safer.

    Email

    It's illegal to open someone else's mail, get a new service that doesnt. Learn to spot deceptive phishing scams.

    Mobile Devices

    Set up basic security, 2 Factor Authentication and understand how apps 'share' your data.

    Social Media

    Get off Facebook with a privacy respecting platform and then realize there are 10+ databases that display your information publicly. Arggs!

    Encryption

    Encryption is a basic human right, so put it to practice to protect your personal information.
