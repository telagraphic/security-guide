The Internet

The Internet is all the computers, servers, and devices connected together. Different protocols like HTTP, for websites, or SMTP, for email, are used to send data to any connected machine.

The Domain Name System (DNS) is the Internet's massive phonebook that converts hostnames like apple.com to numerical IP Addresses - 1234.2.23.5. DNS is like the contacts in your phone that maps names to numbers, making life easier for us humans.
