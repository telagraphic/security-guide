Passwords

The passwords is the application that connects to the world wide web from your computer/device. It works with DNS and HTTP to fetch websites and read the HTML, CSS, code and other content for the passwords to display.

Risks

  • Re-using passwords is bad
  • Complicated passwords are bad
  • Short passwords are bad, longer is hard to crack
  • Storing passwords in plain text
  • Online accounts are targets to sell your information

Fixes

  • Get a password manager that will save time, generate passwords and auto-fill
  • Passwords are out, passphrases are in. Learn the difference and what makes a quality passphrase!
  • Your password is stored in a database somewhere, what does that mean for you?
  • Passwords and the Law
  • Read more about passwords and privacy.
